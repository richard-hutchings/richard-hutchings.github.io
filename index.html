Intro

Greetings! Welcome to my Portfolio


Hi
My name is Richard Hutchings,

I am a self taught coder, who got the coding bug back in early 2018, through a desire to discover the ins and outs of Blockchain technology. I quickly fell in love with coding, learning the basics, such as C Programming, HTML and CSS.
Javascript and the front-end libraries soon became my next focus, and I have studied many courses, tutorials, docs, books and blogs in an effort to understand the inner workings as well as i can.

I really enjoying working with the Front-end technologies, in particular React and Bootstrap and enjoy working with a great team that allows me to further my skills and to be a part of something bigger.

In essence, I love the world of programming, with it's quick growing, constantly changing nature and constant diversity and I look forward to working with awesome people to help build the projects of the coming future.
