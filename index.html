Greetings! Welcome to my Portfolio


Hi there

My name is Richard Hutchings

I’m a versatile web developer with hands-on experience across front-end and full-stack development, working in both government and SaaS settings.
I enjoy building clean, responsive web applications using technologies like HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, Next.js and Vue.js—and I’ve deployed modern sites using tools like Vercel and Netlify.
My toolkit includes Drupal and HubSpot for content management, PostgreSQL and MongoDB on the data side, and backend work with Node.js and C#.
I’ve also worked with Supabase for real-time data handling and authentication, Figma for Wireframes, restfulAPI's, JSON and GeoJSON files, Google Maps features and #D modelling, and have built many component-based UIs.

I have a mobile first approach with my builds as well as being a big believer in good UX, and performance as front of mind.
I have practical experience with website deployment workflows, including managing CNAME records, DNS settings, and working with various hosting providers.
I also bring experience in SEO and Google Analytics, helping ensure the projects I build aren’t just functional and user-friendly, but also findable and measurable.

I enjoy what I do, and I’m always looking for projects that blend good tech with a clear purpose.
