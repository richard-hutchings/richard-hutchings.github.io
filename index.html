Intro

Greetings! Welcome to my Portfolio

My name is Richard Hutchings, I am a self taught coder who began learning to code in May of 2018, and have been coding everyday since.
I was originally a Landscape Gardener who owned and operatored a successful Garden Maintenance business for 10 years, but due to increasing hot summers and re-ocurring work related injuries i decided i needed a career less stressful on the body.


I love coding and the creativity and design elements involved in putting together Web Pages and Apps. As well as the functionality and responsivness that puts them together.
I also especially love the quick changing nature of the industry, and the fact there is always other programming languages and tricks to learn.
I enjoy HTML, CSS, C, Javascript, Bootstrap , JQuery, but React is my favourite language to use at present.

You can check out my Projects here.
