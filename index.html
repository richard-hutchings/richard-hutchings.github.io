Greetings! Welcome to my Portfolio


Hi,

I'm Richard Hutchings — a versatile Software Developer with experience across front-end and full-stack development in both government and SaaS environments.

I enjoy crafting clean, responsive web applications using modern technologies like HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, Next.js, and Vue.js. I’ve deployed scalable, user-focused sites through platforms like Vercel and Netlify, and I’m comfortable working with CMS tools such as Drupal and HubSpot on the content side, and PostgreSQL, MongoDB, Node.js, and C# on the backend.

My approach is mobile-first and performance-driven, with a strong focus on user experience. I have hands-on experience with Supabase for real-time functionality, API integrations, data formats like JSON and GeoJSON, and visual features like Maps and Charts. I also use Figma for wireframes and design collaboration. From managing DNS and CNAME records to optimising for SEO and Google Analytics, I understand how to build projects that not only function well but are also discoverable and measurable.

I love what I do and am always interested in meaningful projects that combine thoughtful technology with solutions that make positive change.
